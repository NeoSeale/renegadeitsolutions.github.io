Our Services:

  1. Windows, Server & Server Core Administration: Our experts have a deep understanding of Windows environments, enabling us to create and manage domains, workgroups, and user identities. Whether you need to set up a new server or streamline your existing network, we've got you covered.
  2. Linux Administration (CentOS & Ubuntu): Embrace the power of open-source with our Linux administration services. We'll help you manage users, files, and permissions using command-line tools, ensuring a secure and efficient environment.
  3. Network Setup & Configuration: Our networking wizards will design and implement a robust network infrastructure tailored to your specific needs. We believe that a well-optimized network is the backbone of any successful organization.
  4. Network Designing with Packet Tracer: Visualize your network layout and configurations before implementation with our cutting-edge Packet Tracer expertise. We can create virtual network simulations to optimize your network design and avoid potential pitfalls.
  5. Remote Desktop Client Support: Need instant support? Our remote desktop services enable us to troubleshoot issues quickly, minimizing downtime and maximizing productivity.
  6. Virtualization - Creating and Managing VM's: Embrace the benefits of virtualization with our expert guidance. We'll assist you in creating and managing virtual machines, enabling you to run multiple systems on a single physical server.
  7. Configuring DNS & DHCP: Stay connected without hassle. Our experts will configure your DNS and DHCP settings, ensuring seamless network connectivity and easy IP management.
  8. Troubleshooting: Technology can be unpredictable, but fear not! Our skilled troubleshooters will diagnose and resolve any issues that arise, ensuring your systems run smoothly at all times.
  9. Front-end Web Development: Need an appealing web presence? We've got the creativity and expertise to craft a stunning front-end for your website, leaving a lasting impression on your audience.
